Classic Tiramisu

Here's how to make tiramisu, step-by-step. Made with egg yolks, sugar, rum, mascarpone,
and whipped cream, then layered with coffee-dipped ladyfingers, this classic tiramisu recipe
is a great make-ahead dessert for holiday gatherings or special occasions.

Ingerdients:

  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 1 cup (200g) sugar, divided
  • 3 ounces (6 tablespoons) dark rum, divided
  • 1 1/2 cups (12 ounces) mascarpone cheese, cold from the fridge
  • 1 cup heavy cream, cold from the fridge
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1 tablespoon instant espresso powder
  • 1 (7-ounce) package ladyfingers
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder (not Dutch-processed) for dusting the top
